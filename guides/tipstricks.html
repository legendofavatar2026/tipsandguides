General Tips & Tricks

Updated July 2026

🎉 Event Tips

Suspicious Goblin Event

This is one of the biggest diamond sink events in the game. Prepare approximately 20,000 Diamonds per day for 3 consecutive days if you plan to purchase every available item.

Recurring Events (Resources Carry Over)

  • Mystic Ore Rush — Around 40,000 Mystic Ores to complete Rounds 1–4.
  • Guardian Star Rush — Around 180,000 Guardian Stones to complete Rounds 1–4.
  • Relic Enhancement Rush — Around 12,000 Relics to complete Rounds 1–4.
  • Emblem Identification Rush — Around 1,000 Emblem Shards to complete Rounds 1–4.
  • Skill Summon Rush — Around 10,000 Skill Summons.
  • Pet Summon Rush — Around 10,000 Pet Summons.
  • Call of Red Dragon — Lucky Coins carry over to future events.
  • World Boss — Vision Forge Crystals carry over between World Boss seasons.

📈 Progression Tips

  • Clear at least 50 floors in every Infinite Tower to unlock the basic collection bonuses from all three tower weapons.
  • Collect every Relic first, then level them evenly to maximize their Owned Effects.
  • For Accessories, level Bonus ATK / DEF / HP to Level 100 before focusing on Boss Damage.
  • For Mine progression, always consume your Common Pickaxes first to level the mine faster and unlock additional mining slots.
  • When obtaining a new Mythic Avatar, activate it for the Collection bonus first. Duplicate copies should be used for rerolling or Transcendence.
  • Training Hourglasses provide the most value when one Hourglass grants one or more level-ups. If you're pushing stages, save accumulated Training EXP until you reach a progression wall.
  • Join an active guild. Guild activities provide a steady source of Diamonds, Summon Scrolls, Mystic Ores, and many other valuable resources.

⚔️ Boss Relic Swap Trick

If you're struggling against the 5th, 8th, or 10th Stage Bosses, try swapping Relics during the fight.

  1. Start the battle with your Boss Damage Relic.
  2. Swap to your Normal Attack Relic at:
  • 33 seconds
  • 23 seconds
  • 13 seconds
  • 3 seconds

Alternate between the two relics at each timing.

🏟️ Arena Tips

  • Use your Arena tickets shortly before Arena reset. This reduces the chance of stronger players finding and attacking you after they've finished spending their own tickets.
  • Arena Ticket Stacking: Purchase 5 tickets from the Arena Shop and 5 more from the Guild Shop, giving you a total of 15/5 tickets. After the daily reset, your stacked tickets remain, allowing you to continue accumulating extra attempts each day.

💡 Final Advice

Legend of Avatar rewards long-term planning more than short-term spending. Saving resources for recurring events, investing in permanent collection bonuses, and joining an active guild will dramatically improve your overall progression.
