Pantheon Guide 👑

Updated July 2026

Introduction

This guide is based on the strategy used by OP Zik [LOA] to become the Ruler of their server's Pantheon. It focuses on the most important missions that commonly prevent players from finishing Pantheon first.

The missions not mentioned here are generally straightforward and will naturally be completed through normal gameplay.

Understanding Pantheon

One of the most common questions is:

"When does Pantheon end?"

The answer is never.

  • Pantheon is a permanent progression feature.
  • It functions like a race between players.
  • The first player to complete every mission becomes Rank 1, followed by Rank 2, Rank 3, and so on.
  • Once earned, Pantheon rankings are permanent and never reset.

Arena Missions

1. Reach 2,150 Arena Points

This is one of the most difficult Arena missions and requires preparation rather than brute force.

  • Save approximately 130–140 Arena Tickets.
  • Do not spend any Arena Tickets while saving.
  • Ignore the daily 5 free Arena attempts during this period.
  • Purchase 5 Arena Tickets with Diamonds and 5 from the Guild Shop every day.
  • This allows you to save around 10 tickets daily, reaching your goal in roughly two weeks.

Once you've accumulated enough tickets, use them during hours when most players are inactive (late at night or after midnight). This reduces the chance of other players noticing your climb and interrupting your attempt.

2. Complete 550 Arena Attempts

Although this mission is simple, it takes a long time to finish.

Complete the 2,150 Arena Point mission first. Afterward, resume using:

  • 5 Daily Attempts
  • 5 Diamond Purchases
  • 5 Guild Shop Tickets

This gives you 15 Arena battles per day and speeds up completion of the total attempt mission.

Rune Missions

The final Rune mission requires obtaining a Level 150 Rune. This is typically the last mission completed by almost every Pantheon finisher due to extremely heavy RNG.

Save Your Rune Keys

  • Purchase the Daily and Weekly Rune Keys.
  • Do not claim them immediately.
  • Leave them in your mailbox until your character becomes stronger.
  • Higher Rune Dungeon difficulty results in better Rune rewards when those saved keys are finally used.

Upgrading a Rune from Level 149 → 150 requires 1,684 runes per attempt, making long-term planning extremely valuable.

Buy World Boss Rune Packs

Always purchase Rune Packs during World Boss events whenever possible.

  • Each pack contains 3,500 runes.
  • You can purchase up to 3 packs, totaling 10,500 runes.

World Boss Rune Types:

  • 🕷️ Iron Spider — Counter & Combo Damage Resistance
  • 🌿 Poison Ivy — Skill Damage Resistance & Double Combo
  • 🌊 Abyssal Tyrant — Skill Critical Hit & Counter Damage Resistance

Reality of the Pantheon Race

Competing for a Pantheon placement generally requires spending resources.

While it isn't impossible for a free-to-play player to become Ruler, doing so is extremely unlikely unless the server has very little competition.

Hidden Progress Strategy

One of the biggest advantages in the Pantheon race is keeping your progress hidden.

Even after completing Pantheon missions, do not immediately claim them.

Unclaimed missions do not increase your Pantheon points, allowing your ranking to remain hidden from other players.

Many players actively monitor Pantheon rankings and adjust their pace based on nearby competitors. By hiding your progress, you can encourage opponents to become less cautious.

Once you've completed every mission—including obtaining your Level 150 Rune—claim all completed missions as quickly as possible.

According to the author's experience, they climbed from approximately 8th–9th place to Ruler in a single day using this strategy.

Final Tips

  • Patience and preparation are more important than rushing missions.
  • Expect heavy RNG when upgrading your Level 150 Rune.
  • Saving rewards and revealing your progress at the right moment can be just as important as raw power.
  • Good luck in your Pantheon race!
