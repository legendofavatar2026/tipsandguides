Gold Farming Guide 💰

Updated July 2026

Introduction

This is a basic F2P guide on how to farm more gold. Feel free to follow it at your own discretion.

This guide is intended for players below Stage 1200. The methods have not been tested beyond Stage 1200.

Recommended Skills ⚔️

Remove Animated Skills

  • Strike
  • Demolition
  • Crush
  • Fury
  • ...and other animated skills.

Use Non-Animated Skills

  • Strength
  • Explosion
  • Acceleration
  • Snipe
  • ...and other non-animated skills.

Recommended Pet 🐶

  • Equip pets whose equipped effect grants Movement Speed.

Why This Works

Using non-animated skills allows your character to perform more auto attacks, helping clear enemy waves more quickly.

Movement Speed from pets helps your character reach the next wave faster.

Faster wave clear = More monsters defeated = More gold earned.

Additional Tips

Staying online and AFK 24/7 provides better rewards than remaining offline.

If you don't want to leave your phone running continuously, consider using LDPlayer or another Android emulator on your PC.

Hope this guide helped you. Happy farming! 😊
