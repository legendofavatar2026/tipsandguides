Relic Swap Guide 🧿

Updated July 2026

Introduction

Relic swapping is an advanced Stage Boss strategy that takes advantage of passive skill windows to maximize your overall damage output.

By switching between two relics at specific times, you can benefit from stronger Normal Attack scaling during passive uptime while maintaining Boss Damage bonuses outside of those windows.

Required Relics

  • 🟣 Mask of the Forgotten Emperor (Purple)
  • 🔵 Mask of Insight (Blue)

Passive Skill Timing

Most passive skills follow the same activation cycle:

  • 20-second cooldown
  • 12-second duration

This creates two important burst windows during every Stage Boss battle:

  • 🟢 33 → 21 seconds remaining
  • 🟢 13 → 1 second remaining

Relic Swap Rotation

Start the battle with the Mask of the Forgotten Emperor equipped.

Time Remaining Action
Start 🟣 Equip Mask of the Forgotten Emperor
33s 🔵 Switch to Mask of Insight
21s 🟣 Switch back to Mask of the Forgotten Emperor
13s 🔵 Switch to Mask of Insight

Why It Works

During passive skill uptime, Mask of Insight provides better value by enhancing Normal Attack-based damage and Combo scaling.

Outside those windows, Mask of the Forgotten Emperor offers stronger Boss Damage bonuses, making it the better choice for sustained damage.

Swapping relics at the correct moments allows each relic to perform during the phase where it is most effective.

Tips

  • Practice the timing until the swaps become consistent.
  • If your damage phase becomes desynchronized, retry the battle.
  • This strategy is most beneficial against Stage Bosses with longer fights.
  • Using presets or quick equipment switching can make relic swaps easier.

Quick Summary

  • 🟣 Start with Mask of the Forgotten Emperor.
  • 🔵 Swap to Mask of Insight at 33 seconds.
  • 🟣 Swap back at 21 seconds.
  • 🔵 Swap again at 13 seconds.
  • ⚔️ Blue Mask for passive burst windows, Purple Mask for Boss Damage outside those windows.
