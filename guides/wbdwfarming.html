World Boss Divine Weapon Farming Guide

Updated July 2026

Introduction

This guide is intended for free-to-play (F2P) players who want to maximize their chances of obtaining World Boss Divine Weapons while spending as few Crystals as possible.

Instead of chasing rankings every rotation, this strategy focuses on reaching the guaranteed 60.4K Forge Score (Level 98) milestone by carefully choosing which World Boss to invest in.

World Boss Rotation

  • 🕷️ Iron Spider → Plasma Edge (Counter DMG)
  • 🌿 Poison Ivy → Epee Fleur (Double Combo DMG)
  • 🌊 Abyssal Tyrant → Tidal Wave (Skill Crit DMG)

The guaranteed Divine Weapon is obtained upon reaching the 60.4K Forge Score (Level 98) milestone.

Step 1 — Skip Iron Spider

Iron Spider's Divine Weapon has the most limited use among the three, making it the ideal World Boss to skip during your first rotation.

  • Complete all World Boss missions.
  • Spend 450 Crystals for the 850 Forge Score mission.
  • This returns 1,000 Crystals, resulting in a net profit of 550 Crystals.
  • Save every remaining Crystal.

By the end of the rotation, you should have accumulated roughly 18,000 Crystals. Ignore rankings and continue saving.

Step 2 — Go All-In on Poison Ivy

After completing both the Iron Spider and Poison Ivy rotations, you'll have approximately 36,000 Crystals, which is generally enough to reach the guaranteed Divine Weapon milestone.

Why Choose Epee Fleur?

  • ✅ +30% Double Combo Damage (Owned Effect)
  • ✅ Excellent alternative if you don't own Accelerator or Solar Halo.

Forge Rolling Strategy

Your goal is to increase Forge Score, not your World Boss ranking.

Keep every Forge slot unlocked.

Every locked slot doubles the Crystal cost:

2 → 4 → 8 → 16 → 32 → 64 → 128 Crystals

Rolling with all slots open keeps every roll at just 2 Crystals, making it the most efficient Crystal-to-Forge Score method.

Recommended Rotation

1st Rotation

  • ❌ Iron Spider — Skip
  • ✅ Poison Ivy — Get
  • ❌ Abyssal Tyrant — Skip

2nd Rotation

  • ✅ Iron Spider — Get
  • ❌ Poison Ivy — Skip
  • ✅ Abyssal Tyrant — Get

Alternating your target World Boss each rotation allows you to recover your Crystal reserves while steadily collecting every World Boss Divine Weapon over time.

⚠️ RNG still applies. This strategy greatly improves your chances but cannot guarantee perfect luck every rotation.

Alternative F2P Goal — Poison Ivy Wings

If you prefer climbing the World Boss rankings instead of securing the guaranteed Divine Weapon, you can aim to purchase Poison Ivy Wings (Budding Wings) from the Black Market.

Cost

🪽 10 Immortal Fragments

How to Obtain 10 Immortal Fragments (First Month)

  • 🏆 Top 20 in one World Boss → 4 Fragments
  • 🏆 Top 50 in two World Bosses → 3 Fragments × 2 = 6 Fragments

Total: 10 Immortal Fragments, enough to immediately purchase the wings.

Wing Priority

  1. Poison Ivy Wings ⭐ (Highest Priority)
  2. Iron Spider Wings
  3. Abyssal Tyrant Wings

Why Prioritize Poison Ivy Wings?

  • ✅ Excellent for stage progression.
  • ✅ Great temporary replacement until obtaining Pantheon Wings.

This approach is ideal for players who want to obtain the wings during the first World Boss rotation. Afterward, you can switch to the Divine Weapon farming strategy for future rotations.
