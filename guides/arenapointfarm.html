Arena Point Farming Guide ⚔️

Updated July 2026

Introduction

This strategy is designed for players who are the 2nd or 3rd strongest on their server—strong enough to consistently defeat the players below them, but not strong enough to challenge Rank #1.

It also works well if you're already Rank #1 and simply want to maintain a comfortable lead before the weekly Arena reset.

🌱 "Leveling the Grass"

Before spending your Arena tickets, check the Arena Points of the players ranked directly below you.

For example, if you're Rank #3, monitor the players in Ranks #4, #5, and #6.

Begin by repeatedly attacking Rank #4. If their Arena Points fall below Rank #5, continue attacking whoever becomes the new Rank #4.

Repeat this process throughout your Arena session.

The objective is to keep every player below you at nearly the same Arena Point total, making it much harder for any single player to build enough points to overtake your position.

🛡️ Creating a Buffer Zone

By constantly rotating attacks on whoever occupies Rank #4, you'll gradually create a large point gap between yourself and everyone below.

If another player decides to push Arena later, they'll first need to recover all of the points you've taken from them before they can even challenge your ranking.

This buffer makes it significantly easier to maintain your position until the weekly reset.

⏰ Best Time to Use This Strategy

The ideal time to farm Arena Points is around 30 minutes before the daily Arena reset, after most players have already used their Arena tickets.

Avoid doing this immediately after reset. If you lower another player's score early in the day, they'll still have plenty of time to recover the lost points.

Likewise, if you've already reached Rank #2 and know you have little chance of defeating Rank #1, it's usually better to stop rather than waste additional Arena tickets.

💎 Is It Worth It?

This strategy requires refreshing your Arena challenge list, which costs Diamonds.

Only invest Diamonds if the weekly Arena rewards are valuable to you.

The Shield skill obtained from Arena may not seem particularly strong early in the game, but at higher progression (around Stage 1300+), higher Shield levels provide significantly larger shields, making Arena rewards a worthwhile long-term investment.
