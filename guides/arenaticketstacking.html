Arena Ticket Stacking Guide 🎟️

Updated July 2026

Introduction

Arena Ticket stacking allows you to save extra tickets above the normal limit. Understanding how the daily reset works will help you avoid losing purchased tickets and prepare for future Arena pushes.

How the Daily Reset Works

The Arena system only refills your tickets if you have 5 or fewer tickets at reset.

  • 5/5 or below → Reset to 5/5.
  • Above 5/5 → Your ticket count is preserved.

Examples

Before Reset After Reset
4/5 5/5 ❌
5/5 5/5 ❌
6/5 6/5 ✅
7/5 7/5 ✅

To stack tickets successfully, always make sure your total is above 5 before the daily reset.

How to Stack Tickets

  1. Start with your daily Arena Tickets.
  2. Use enough tickets to create space.
  3. Purchase additional Arena Tickets.
  4. Finish with more than 5 tickets before reset.

Example:

  • Start: 5/5
  • Use 3 → 2/5
  • Buy 5 → 7/5
  • Daily Reset → 7/5 ✅

You can repeat this process to continue building your ticket stockpile.

Important Tips

  • Never finish the day with 5 or fewer tickets if you're trying to stack.
  • Always end above 5 tickets before the daily reset.
  • Plan ahead if you're saving tickets for Arena events or ranking pushes.

Summary

  • 🎟️ Ticket stacking only works above the normal cap.
  • 🔄 The daily reset restores tickets only if you have 5 or fewer.
  • 📈 Keep more than 5 tickets to preserve your stockpile.
  • ⚠️ Careful planning prevents wasting purchased Arena Tickets.
