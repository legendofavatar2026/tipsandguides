Beginner Guide: Growth & Idle Rewards 🌱

Updated July 2026

Introduction

If you're just starting your adventure in Legend of Avatar, one of the easiest ways to grow faster is by timing when you claim your Idle Rewards.

Following this simple strategy can significantly increase the rewards you receive during your early progression.

Push Before Claiming

As long as your avatar is still strong enough to clear the next milestone stage, do not claim your Idle Rewards yet.

Milestone stages include:

  • 10
  • 20
  • 30
  • 50
  • 60
  • 70
  • ...and other milestone stages.

Once you clear the next milestone, your Idle Rewards can increase by approximately 4×–5×.

If you're still able to progress, keep pushing before collecting your rewards.

When Should You Claim?

Only claim your Idle Rewards as a last resort, when your avatar is no longer able to clear the next milestone stage.

This ensures you receive the highest possible rewards for your current progression.

How to Push Further

If you're stuck just before the next milestone, consider using some of your upgrade resources to gain enough power to progress.

  • Avatar Scrolls — Chance to obtain stronger avatars, including Mythic ones.
  • Skill Scrolls and Pet Scrolls — Use them if you're not currently saving for an event.
  • Relics, Guardian Stars, and Mythic Ores — Invest in upgrades if they're enough to help you clear the next milestone and you're not saving them for future events.

Sometimes a single upgrade is all you need to unlock the next milestone and dramatically improve your Idle Rewards.

Recommended Investment

If you plan to play the game long-term, the Ad Removal Card is one of the best purchases available.

Benefits include:

  • ✅ Saves time.
  • ✅ Removes ad interruptions.
  • ✅ Permanent account benefit.

For active players, it's one of the highest-value purchases in the game.

Key Takeaway

Push first. Claim later.

Maximizing your stage progression before collecting Idle Rewards is one of the simplest and most effective ways for new players to accelerate their growth.
