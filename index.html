Tips & Tricks

Event Tips

  • Suspicious Goblin Event is a diamond burning event. You will need around 20,000 diamonds per day for 3 days to purchase all available items.

Recurring Events (Resources Carry Over)

  • Mystic Ore Rush Event – Around 40,000 ores needed to complete all missions from Round 1–4.
  • Guardian Star Rush Event – Around 180,000 guardian stones needed to complete all missions from Round 1–4.
  • Relic Enhancement Rush Event – Around 12,000 relics needed to complete all missions from Round 1–4.
  • Skill Summon Rush Event – Around 10,000 summons needed to finish all missions.
  • Pet Summon Rush Event – Around 10,000 summons needed to finish all missions.
  • Call of Red Dragon – Lucky Coins from this event carry over to the next event.
  • World Boss – Vision Forge Crystals carry over to the next world boss.

Progression Tips

  • For Infinite Tower, it is recommended to clear at least 50 floors on each tower to obtain the basic collection bonus from the three weapons.
  • For Relics, its better to get all the types of relics, the equally level each relich for the maximum owned effect.
  • For Accessory, you level Bonus ATK/DEF/HP to level100, then you focus on leveling Boss Damage to max.
  • For Forge, if you are SS Tier below its talked that getting a 4x Final Double Combo Dmg & 1x Final Atk.(this is subject to debate as everyone is unique) For SSS Tier 3xFinal DCD;2xFinal Boss Dmg.
  • For Mine, use your common pickaxe first for better/faster leveling of your mine to open more slots.
  • For Mythic Avatar, if you get a new Avatar its best to confirm it for collection, if its dupe/copy reroll or use it to transcend your best mythic.
